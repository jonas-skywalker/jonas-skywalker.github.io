jonas-skywalker Github pages:

Golden Ratio Flower

golden_ratio-flower

A little interactive exploration of drawing a flower with different rational and irrational numbers in the browser with the p5.js library (inspired by a numberphile video). Numberphile video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sj8Sg8qnjOg I highly suggest watching the video as it will give a good explanation of the mathematical background of the behaviour of this flower construct. You can interact with this at jonas-skywalker.github.io

Complex squares/Mandelbrot exploration

complex_squares

Mandelbrot exploration (Drag around the red dot to see how the complex number behaves and if it stays in the mandelbrot set)

Numberline squares/Mandelbrot exploration

numberline_squares

See how numbers on the real numberline behave if you square them over and over again.

Feigenbaum fractal

feigenbaum

Visualisation of the Feigenbaum fractal in the browser.

Ulam Primes

ulam-primes

A small representation of the ulam primes within the browser using the p5.js library. This project is inspired by a numberphile video wich explains the mathematical background of this project (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iFuR97YcSLM).
